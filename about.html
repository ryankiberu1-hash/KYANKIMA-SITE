Our Mission

Founded in 2019 by 47 women in Mukono District, Uganda, Kyankima Women’s Cooperative empowers women through agriculture, handcrafts, and health awareness.

Our Story

We began as a small group sharing skills and resources. Today, we are a registered community-based organization with a growing market reach.
